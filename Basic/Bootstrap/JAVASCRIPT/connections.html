Salem (pronounced [seÀêlam] (listen)),
is a major city in Salem district, located on the banks of Thirumanimutharu river in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
Salem is the sixth largest urban agglomeration and metropolitan city in the state by population next to Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Tiruchirappalli and Tiruppur and the sixth largest city in Tamil Nadu by area covering 124 km2 (48 sq mi).[5]
